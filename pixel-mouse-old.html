PIXELMOUSE PIXELMOUSE PIXELMOUSE

Pixelmouse is a custom game created for the game controllers. This game has a total of 3 maze layouts. The first two are public, and you are free to model your game controller based on the restrictions and nuisances of the map. The last maze layout is private, and will be played as a final round for the game tournament.

Maze Levels

The first maze level is open to the public. You are allowed to download this level to calibrate your hardware to the movement of the software mouse. The second maze level is visible to the public, but is not available for download. The third maze level is private and will be played as a final round for the game tournament.
